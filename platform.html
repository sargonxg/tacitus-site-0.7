02. INGESTION VECTORS

>> Data Infrastructure

Pipeline A: Velocity

TARGET: Web Intelligence

Captures high-velocity updates via Tavily/Serper APIs and custom news scrapers (Reuters, AP, Al Jazeera). Deduplicates content and timestamps events within 3-12 hour refresh cycles.

Pipeline B: Specificity

TARGET: Curated Profiles

Targeting hard-to-find local sources (e.g., Enab Baladi, Congo Research Group). Uses Firecrawl to extract text from local radio transcripts and NGO updates.

Pipeline C: Authority

TARGET: Strategic RAG

A vector repository of slow-moving, high-authority documents (UN SG Reports, Carnegie, ICG). Provides historical continuity and legal grounding.

03. AGENTIC PROCESSING

>> The "Concordia" Swarm

Tacitus does not summarize; it reconstructs. A council of specialized AI agents processes the ingested data in three sequential layers.

LAYER 1: FACT DISCOVERY

Developments Agent

Synthesizes last 72h of military, political, and humanitarian movements.

Contradiction Checker

Cross-references sources to flag propaganda, missing dates, or low-reliability origins.

LAYER 2: DEEP ANALYSIS

Ontology Engine

Maps actors, grievances, and alliances into a graph database. Identifies "spoilers" and windows for mediation.

Narrative Miner

Analyzes framing and legitimacy claims. Distinguishes between "Drivers of Conflict" (Resource/Ideological) and surface rhetoric.

LAYER 3: POLICY GENERATION

> INPUT: User selects Stakeholder (e.g., "UN OCHA", "UK FCDO", "NGO").

> PROCESS: Agent aligns findings with stakeholder mandate, leverage, and risk appetite.

> OUTPUT: "Prioritize community acceptance over speed; negotiate humanitarian corridors via Actor X."
