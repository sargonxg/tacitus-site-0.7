TACITUS LAB

>> The Habermas Machine

Tacitus is built on the hypothesis that AI can act as a "mediating layer" that structures democratic deliberation. We do not invent these concepts; we operationalize them for enterprise and diplomatic data.

Foundational Research

Our architecture is informed by recent breakthroughs in Deliberative AI:

  • AI for Democratic Deliberation
    Google DeepMind & Oxford (2024)

    Demonstrating how "Habermas Machines" can help groups find common ground statements that are preferred to those written by human mediators.

  • Constitutional AI
    Anthropic

    Utilizing a set of principles (a constitution) to guide AI behavior, ensuring conflict resolution remains within ethical bounds.

Our Contributions

Tacitus Lab focuses on the "Last Mile" of conflict data:

  • > Narrative Graphing: Mapping not just facts, but "sacred values" in negotiation.
  • > Email-Native Ingestion: Applying deliberative structures to unstructured SMTP data streams.
  • > Privacy-Preserving RAG: Analyzing sensitive HR data without training models on it.
